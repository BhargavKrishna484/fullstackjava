About Department

DEPARTMENT OF ELECTRONICS AND COMMUNICARION ENGINEERING

The Electronics and Communications Engineering Department at SVIET College is dedicated to fostering excellence in the field. With a focus on electronics and communications, our program equips students with the knowledge and skills to tackle the ever-evolving technological landscape. Through a combination of theory, practical applications, and hands-on experience, students gain expertise in areas such as signal processing, telecommunications, embedded systems, and more. Our department emphasizes innovation, problem-solving, and critical thinking, empowering students to contribute to cutting-edge advancements in the field. By joining the Electronics and Communications Engineering Department at SVIET College, students embark on a journey of limitless opportunities in the dynamic world of technology. .

Aim Of The Department

To give the students real-time experience and help them learn the true functional elements of ELECTRONICS & COMMUNICATION.

The department has always been at the forefront in producing the quality engineers.

To give an extra mileage to the students by training them on software design and hardware-software integration so as to create more space for them in the job market.

OUR VISION

To become centre of excellence in Electronics and Communication Engineering to meet the challenges of industry and the society.

MISSON

DM1: Impart high quality education to enable students to face challenges of Electronics and Communication Engineering.

DM2: Provide all possible support to promote activities in the related areas of VLSI,Comminications,Signal Processing,and Micro Processors&Micro Controllers.

DM3: Inculate ethical,professional values and life learning skills to address the sicietal needs.
